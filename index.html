ABOUT ME

I am Maryam Adekoya, a Lagos based web developer, content writer and entrepreneur. I studied Economics at the University of Lagos but fell in love with Web development. My love for business and entrepreneurship led me to co-founding Gidi Burger with my friend. I am passionate about educating the girl child and for this cause, I will be launching the #Flygirlcode platform soon to help girls develop their coding skills.

My writing combines ideas from a wide range of disciplines but what interests me most is business and entrepreneurship from a personal point of view. In the coming years, I want to explore all i can about web dev, and software programming. I am a strong believer in developing ones skills just as many skill-based opportunities keep popping up. I also enjoy sourcing for promotional, paraphernalias, teaching, trying out D.I.Y interior projects, Surfing the internet and reading.
